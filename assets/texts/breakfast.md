# Breakfast Recipes

### Mini Waffle Maker Banana Waffles
Ingredients:
- 3/4 cup AP flour (96g)
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/8 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 cup mashed banana (100g, about 1 banana)
- 1/3 cup milk (75g)
- 2 tsp brown sugar
- 1 large egg
- 1 Tbsp oil
Instructions:
> Add oil to mini waffle maker  
> Four mins each waffle
> Makes 4 waffles
