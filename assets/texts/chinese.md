# Chinese Food Recipes

## Mapo Tofu
Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground pork
- 1 box 10oz? firm tofu
- Sauce:
- 1 Tbsp miso paste
- 1 tsp tomato paste (or vegan oyster sauce)
- 1 tsp toubanjang
- 2 tsp sesame oil
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1/3 cup rice wine
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/2 cornstarch

Instructions:
> In medium pot, medium high heat, brown ground pork, break into small pieces  
> Turn down to medium heat, add sauce  
> Add tofu, cover
